As

.
Se

um

,

,

relevantes

,

uma

de
bem-estar
,
equilíbrio

que
contribuem
para

uma

o

com
as

e
aprender
.
Este
desafio

complexo
nos
contextos
em
que
as

educacionais
trabalham
com

em
situações
de
vulnerabilidade
social
.
Há
evidências
de
que
há
altos
níveis
de
sofrimento
entre

que

,

de

e

.
Há

para

o

.
A

.
As

.

Issue
Rationale
Educação
Sociedade
&
Culturas
welcomes
articles
that
bring
a
vision
of
possibility
in
the
educational
intervention
with
young
people
through
the
arts
,
in
and
out
of
school
.
Of
particular
interest
are
studies
that
capture
promising
multidisciplinary
experiences
in
promoting
wellbeing
and
the
educational
and
social
inclusion
of
vulnerable
young
people
.
Visual
arts
,
music
and
drama
have
proven
to
be
important
tools
in
the
teaching-learning
processes
.
If
they
have
a
very
specific
role
in
themselves
by
promoting
self-expression
,
creativity
and
the
disciplinary
knowledge
they
imply
,
arts
can
also
become
relevant
for
educational
success
,
as
a
pedagogical
strategy
,
by
upholding
a
sense
of
wellbeing
,
balance
and
engagement
that
help
build
a
disposition
to
engage
with
others
and
learn
.
This
challenge
becomes
even
more
complex
in
contexts
where
educational
institutions
work
with
young
people
in
situations
of
social
vulnerability
.
There
is
evidence
that
there
are
high
levels
of
suffering
among
the
population
attending
in-school
and
out-of-school
education
,
which
is
not
alien
to
their
living
circumstances
in
the
nearest
and
most
distant
environment
.
There
is
need
for
an
education
that
takes
advantage
of
these
strategies
to
promote
also
the
mastery
of
knowledge
and
skills
required
by
the
current
social
life
.
Adopting
such
approach
implies
the
ability
of
educational
professionals
to
work
together
.
Educational
institutions
are
challenged
to
include
other
professionals
,
such
as
social
workers
,
social
educators
,
psychologists
,
nurses
,
therapists
,
and
occupational
guidance
specialists
in
reducing
ESL
and
promoting
a
school
sense
of
belonging
.
