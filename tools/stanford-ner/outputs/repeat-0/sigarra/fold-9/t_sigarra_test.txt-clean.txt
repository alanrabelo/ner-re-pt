.
Of

that
capture
promising
multidisciplinary

wellbeing

the

social
inclusion

young
people
.
Visual
arts
,
music

proven

teaching-learning
processes
.
If
they
have
a
very
specific
role
in
themselves
by
promoting
self-expression
,

the
disciplinary

they

,

,

,

.
This

There

that
there

suffering

population

in-school

out-of-school

,

and
most
distant
environment
.
There

need
for
an

that

advantage
of

strategies
to

also

knowledge

skills

.
Adopting

ability

professionals

.
Educational
institutions

,

workers
,
social

,
psychologists
,
nurses
,
therapists
,

ESL

a
school

of
belonging
.
